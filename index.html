What is ISUSU?

Isusu is a traditional rotating savings system from West and Central Africa where a group of people contribute money at regular intervals and each person takes a turn collecting the full amount. It teaches patience, consistency, and accountability with your finances.

"Beware of little expenses. A small leak will sink a great ship." Benjamin Franklin

ISUSU the app takes that same energy and puts it in your pocket. Every franc matters when you are a student. Whether it is lunch at the cafeteria, transport across Kigali, or a course textbook that costs more than you expected, this tracker helps you see the full picture. No accounts, no cloud, no sharing your data with anyone. Everything stays on your device.

The goal is simple: know where your money goes so you can make better choices. Start small, stay consistent, and watch your habits change.

Settings

Manage your categories, spending cap, currencies, and data.

Categories

Add or remove spending categories. Removing a category does not delete transactions that already use it.

Spending Cap

Set a monthly target for yourself. The dashboard will show how much is left before you hit it.

Exchange Rates

Set manual conversion rates. Changing these updates the dashboard totals in other currencies.

Import and Export

Back up your transactions as a JSON file. Imported files are validated before they replace your data.

Reset All Data

Clears every transaction you have recorded. This cannot be undone.
